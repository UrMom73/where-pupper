Where's Pupper?!

A Text Adventure By Raju Praju

How to Play

Pupper, your dog, your life, has gone missing and you must find him at all costs! To do so, search areas, find and collect tools, inspect objects for clues, and investigate people! But be wary, there'll be some dangers on your journey! If you ever need help, just ask.

P.S. if your taste is not good enough for this music >:P, you can turn it off using mute. To turn it back on, use unmute.
